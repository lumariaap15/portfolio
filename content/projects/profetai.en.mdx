A price-tracking system for the Argentine market. Pulls SEPA open data, normalizes
heterogeneous product names with embeddings, and surfaces LLM-generated price summaries —
with a Telegram notification layer for price drops.

The design principle driving it: **deterministic logic stays deterministic** (parsing,
matching thresholds, alerts) and the LLM is reserved for the genuinely fuzzy tasks
(normalization, summarization).
